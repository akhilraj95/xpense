Thank you for downloading Xpense. Xpense bot is available at your service on flock.

Help us serve you better by giving us your basic information.

Be smart use /slash commands

/xpense start : to start a new track

/xpense add : to add expense to a track

/xpense status : to view status of all tracked expenses

/xpense delete : to delete saved tracks

/xpense report : to generate reports for saved tracks
